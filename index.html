PATHFINDER

Discover how your mind works.

This is not a normal career test.

Pathfinder analyzes how you think, solve problems, adapt, communicate, and respond to challenges.

Your results help uncover strengths, work styles, career pathways, and hidden abilities.

1. Pattern Recognition

Find hidden systems, causes, and connections.

2. Problem Solving

Work through realistic challenges and choose strong solutions.

3. Mechanical / Hands-On Thinking

Understand tools, processes, physical systems, and practical fixes.

4. People & Communication

Read situations, understand people, and communicate clearly.

5. Creativity & Innovation

Generate ideas, connect concepts, and solve problems in new ways.

6. Leadership & Decision Making

Make choices, prioritize, and guide others through uncertainty.

7. Focus & Detail

Notice mistakes, inconsistencies, and important details.

8. Stress / Pressure Response

Understand how you react when pressure, time, or stakes increase.

9. Persistence / Retry Behavior

Measure effort, recovery, challenge tolerance, and willingness to improve.

10. Work Environment Fit

Identify whether you work best with structure, movement, people, independence, or variety.

11. Career Interest Reality Check

Compare career interests against the real tasks, stress, training, and lifestyle involved.
